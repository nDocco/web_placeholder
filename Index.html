About Us

E.J. & P Doherty Construction Co. Ltd are an established family owned and managed company offering civil engineering, groundwork, and landscaping services throughout the South East of England.

Established in 1962 the company has a long tradition of delivering quality construction on time and on budget.

Our directors have in excess of 70 years combined experience in our trade.

We are committed to adopt a proactive approach within any project team.

We are able to offer advice on design and specification and solutions to most construction problems that may be encountered.

We undertake commercial contracts for private clients, developers and contractors within a 50 mile radius of our base in Benfleet.
