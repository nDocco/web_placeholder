Paving

A well laid block paved driveway remains one of the most attractive surfaces available. Uniting both appealing aesthetics and durable performance block paving is unrivalled in its versatility.

Whatever your scope or budget we can provide a paving solution offering any type of available paving from any manufacturer. (Links from this page will take you to all of the paving options currently available.) Where required SUDS compliant permeable paving can be provided.

Our pavers have been involved in the laying of large scale commercial schemes, prestigious town centre projects as well as numerous private driveways and paths and therefore have experience of laying all types of blocks and paviors in any possible bond or pattern.

Advice is available on complementary colours, and details such as features, edge kerbs, steps etc to provide finishing touches that will help your new driveway complement and enhance your property and raise it above the 'ordinary'.
