Highway Works

We can install domestic and industrial vehicle crossings in all areas of Essex (except in Southend Borough where they must be installed by the current term contractor).

In the past, before local authorities changed their procurement procedures the company carried out highways works for local authorities throughout Essex, London and the surrounding areas.

We were carried term maintenance contracts for Basildon, Thurrock and Havering as well as numerous highway improvements throughout the region

We have maintained £10 million liability insurance cover and trained operatives and supervisors with current accreditation under the New Roads and Streetworks Act (NRSWA).

This enables us to carry out works on the public highway

We can undertake section 278 works in conjunction with any development and can assist in obtaining Sreetwork Licenses.
